2018 MATSim User Meeting

The 2018 MATSim User Meeting will be held on June 23, 2018, in Atlanta, Georgia USA. That is the Saturday before the Transportation Research Board’s bi-annual Innovations in Travel Modeling conference, which is June 24-27, 2018.

  • Abstracts and short papers for ITM were due on December 11, 2017. We encourage anyone interested in MATSim, whether as an end-user, developer, or presenter, to attend the annual meeting!
  • We will coordinate with the main TRB conference so that papers are not presented twice.
  • Register today! Go to EventBrite: MATSim User Meeting 2018

MATSim is an open-source framework for implementing large-scale agent-based transport simulations.
